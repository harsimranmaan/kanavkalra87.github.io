Vivek Singh Memorial Scholarship 2012-13

Dated: Jan 15, 2012

Vivek Singh Memorial Trust introduced a need cum merit based annual scholarship with a holistic approach, viz. Vivek Singh Memorial Scholarship to the students of National Institute of Technology, Durgapur who are pursuing Under Graduate degrees in engineering. The Scholarship is aimed at honoring Vivek's spirit of helping others and will be awarded to academically sound students having weak financial background.
