EOTO 2014

Dated: Apr 04, 2014

Vivek singh memorial trust has disbursed the annual education fee of existing 13 students under the project Each One Teach One. Among them, 4 are in Mother Teresa School Academy, Durgapur, 6 are ni National Education Mission Public School, Noida and 3 are in Bengaluru.Total Annual Fee for the academic year 2013-14 is 65,000/-
