Vivek Singh Memorial Fellowship

Dated: Apr 10, 2014

Vivek Sigh Memorial Trust has undertaken a new initiative to encourage honest social efforts of our NIT Durgapur students - Vivek Singh Memorial Fellowship.

This year, it was awarded to following five students of 2014 batch by our Honorary Prof. Indrajit Basak, NIT Durgapur at the college's national techno-management festival - AAROHAN.

  • Mr. Shivam Rai
  • Ms. Shubham Barskar
  • Ms. Arpita Mrigwani
  • Ms. Shashi Das
