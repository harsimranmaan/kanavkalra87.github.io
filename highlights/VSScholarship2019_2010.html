Scholarship Award: Gaurav Jain

Dated: Apr 08, 2010

Gaurav Jain became the recepient of the inaugural Vivek Singh Memorial Scholarship.Gaurav is student of the Mechanical Engineering(2nd year) at NIT Durgapur. He has an excellent and consistent academic record and is actively involved as a member of the Indian Society for Technical Education(ISTE) and American Society for Technical Education(ASME). He loves reading magazines and strongly believes in Hardwork and Team Spirit.

Gaurav is the winner of the TYCOONS 2009-INDIA'S GREATEST YOUTH LEADERSHIP HUNT at the college stage. On being asked about how he felt about being awarded the scholarship,the boy from Howrah said that he was excited and felt honoured to receive the scholarship and promised to keep up his hard work and move towards shaping his career on the success-path.
